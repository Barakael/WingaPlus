### "Can't see Manage Categories tab"
**Solution:** Only shop owners and storekeepers have access. Contact your shop owner.

### "Can't delete a category"
**Solution:** The category has products assigned. Go to Products, filter by that category, and reassign products to different categories.

### "Categories not loading"
**Solution:** 
1. Refresh the page
2. Check your internet connection
3. Contact support if issue persists

### "Don't see default categories"
**Solution:** Contact system administrator to run the category seeder.
